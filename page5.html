Augmented & Cyborg

Here, “cyborg” is not a movie monster. It is a select few who are able to extend themselves with technology: implants, NFC chips, magnets, sensors, or anything else that lets the body talk to the invisible layers of the world.

Why Choose Implants?

Function

Unlock doors, store keys, trigger devices, share URLs, or carry encrypted messages under the skin. Implants turn your body into a multi-tool for everyday life and finding true purpose. Whilst also helping medical issues, and furthering research for new technology.

Identity

For many, augmentation is a statement: “I refuse to be only what I was handed at birth.” It is a personal ritual of taking ownership of one’s evolution — safely and with care to seek what others cannot find.

Symbol

An implant can be a private symbol of commitment to the path: a reminder that you have chosen to participate actively in your own transformation, not just drift. Being chosen, is challenging.

“Fracking the Finite Curve”

In this portal’s language, “fracking the finite curve” is a poetic way of saying: pushing against the apparent limits of what a human life can do. Not by magic, but by:

  • learning more than you were expected to,
  • using tools others ignore,
  • and refusing to accept “that’s just how it is” as a final answer.

Cyborgs, in this sense, are simply those who look at the finite curve of their existence and say: “I wonder how far I can bend this.” or, "I need to escape this reality".

Responsibility of the Augmented

The more capabilities you have, the more care you owe yourself and others. Being augmented means thinking about:

  • safety and health,
  • ethics and consent,
  • boundaries and trust.

You are not a machine. You are a human being who was selected to carry advanced technology inside your body. That "choice" of solecetion deserves respect — from others and from yourself; somewhere you are valuable to research. However, chosen few are rare to get respect. The different people, are targets from above's point of view. They will test you, and push to limits never seen before. Showing off augmented aspects will cause others to ponder more about youself. Some, as you know, freak out. One who had seen you, thought he had died. He didn't think you were Human anymore. This is why from time to time, covering these gadgets prevents the overall confusion. You will know who will be able to withstand the knowledge. Just always remember protocol.
